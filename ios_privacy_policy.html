隐私政策

生效日期：2026年3月17日  |  光颜相机 LumiCamera

本《隐私政策》旨在说明开发者张广华（以下简称"我们"）如何处理您在使用"光颜相机"移动应用（以下简称"本应用"）时可能涉及的个人信息。

核心摘要：我们不收集、不上传、不存储您的任何个人信息。所有数据和操作均在您的设备本地完成。

1. 我们不收集任何个人信息

本应用是一款纯粹的本地工具应用。我们不会以任何形式收集、上传、存储或与任何第三方分享您的个人信息。

  • 照片与视频：您拍摄或从相册选择的所有照片和视频，仅用于实时处理（如补光效果）或根据您的操作保存在本地相册中。我们无法访问或获取这些内容。
  • 自定义数据：您在各功能中设置的文本、样式、冥想进度等，仅通过 UserDefaults 或应用沙盒文件系统存储在您的设备本地，我们无法访问。
  • 使用分析：本应用不包含任何第三方统计工具或 SDK，不追踪您的任何操作。

2. 网络请求说明

本应用的"音频冥想"和"视觉冥想"功能，会从我们的服务器（lumicamera.cn）加载音频和视频素材文件。此过程：

  • 仅加载静态媒体文件，不涉及任何个人数据的传输；
  • 不会上传您的任何信息至服务器；
  • 您可以在无网络环境下使用本地缓存内容。

3. 我们请求的权限及用途

本应用仅在您主动使用相关功能时才会触发权限请求，您可随时在"设置"中管理。

📷
相机权限（NSCameraUsageDescription）

用于拍照、录像、Live Photo 拍摄、实时预览补光效果，以及"补妆镜"功能。相机数据仅实时使用，不被收集。

🖼️
照片图库权限（NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription）

用于将拍摄内容保存至相册，或让您选择视频作为电子木鱼、冥想、视觉冥想等功能的背景。我们仅在您操作时访问，不扫描相册。

🎙️
麦克风权限（NSMicrophoneUsageDescription）

用于两种场景：① 录像和 Live Photo 时采集音频；② 电子蜡烛、电子烧香、烛光凝视冥想功能中的"吹气检测"（通过检测气流声实现吹灭效果）。音频数据不被上传，仅本地实时处理。

📡
运动与方向权限（NSMotionUsageDescription）

用于电子蜡烛、电子烧香的烟雾随手机倾斜方向飘动效果，以及曼陀罗冥想的重力视差动画。仅读取设备姿态数据，不收集或上传。

4. 数据存储与安全

您在本应用中产生的所有数据（自定义祝福语、视频播放列表、冥想设置等），使用 iOS 标准机制（UserDefaults 或应用沙盒）安全存储在本地设备。这些数据仅您自己可见，卸载应用时将被系统一并删除。

5. 儿童隐私

我们不会有意收集任何未满 14 周岁儿童的个人信息。本应用不以儿童为目标用户。

6. 隐私政策的更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策以反映功能变化或法律要求。重大变更将在应用内通过适当方式通知您，您可在本应用内随时查阅最新版本。

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026  |  LumiCamera

This Privacy Policy explains how developer Zhang Guanghua ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with your use of the LumiCamera mobile application ("this App").

In short: We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal information. All data and operations are processed locally on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

This App is a purely offline utility. We do not collect, upload, store, or share any personal information with any third party.

  • Photos and Videos: All photos and videos you capture or select are used only for real-time processing and saved to your local photo album upon your action. We cannot access or obtain this content.
  • Custom Data: Texts, styles, meditation progress, and settings are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults or the app's sandboxed file system. We cannot access this data.
  • Usage Analytics: This App contains no third-party analytics tools or SDKs. We do not track your activities.

2. Network Requests

The "Sound Meditation" and "Visual Meditation" features load audio and video assets from our server (lumicamera.cn). This process:

  • Only downloads static media files — no personal data is transmitted;
  • Does not upload any information to the server;
  • Cached content remains accessible offline.

3. Permissions We Request and Their Purposes

Permissions are requested only when you actively use related features. You can manage them anytime in your device Settings.

📷
Camera Access (NSCameraUsageDescription)

Required for photo/video shooting, Live Photo capture, real-time fill light preview, and the Makeup Mirror. Camera data is used only in real time and not collected.

🖼️
Photo Library Access (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription)

Required to save captured content to your album, or to let you select videos as backgrounds for Digital Woodfish, Meditation, and Visual Meditation features. We only access it when you perform such actions.

🎙️
Microphone Access (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription)

Used in two scenarios: ① recording audio during video/Live Photo shooting; ② detecting a blow gesture in the Digital Candle, Digital Incense, and Candle Gazing meditation features. Audio is processed locally and never uploaded.

📡
Motion & Orientation (NSMotionUsageDescription)

Used to animate smoke drifting with device tilt in Digital Candle and Digital Incense, and to power the gravity parallax effect in Mandala Meditation. Only device attitude data is read — nothing is collected or uploaded.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data you generate in this App (custom texts, video playlists, meditation settings, etc.) is securely stored locally on your device using iOS standard mechanisms (UserDefaults or the app's sandbox). This data is only visible to you and will be deleted if you uninstall the App.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 14. This App is not directed at children.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the App, and the latest version is always accessible inside the App.
