LumiCamera User Guide

欢迎使用光颜相机！这是一款集成了多种创意拍摄与实用工具的App，旨在为您提供有趣、便捷的使用体验。本手册将引导您探索App的全部功能。

一. 相机与补光

1.1 补光模式

  • 开启与调节：在主拍摄界面，屏幕本身即为补光灯。轻点屏幕任意位置，即可呼出底部的控制面板。您可以通过拖动 “亮度” 和 “透明度” 滑块，实时调整补光效果。
  • 色彩控制：在控制面板中，您可以在 “自定义” 标签页通过色轮自由选择颜色，或在 “预设颜色” 标签页快速应用精心调制的氛围色彩。
  • 悬浮补光 (画中画)：
    1. 切换到控制面板的 “悬浮框” 标签页。
    2. 点击 “开启悬浮框” 按钮。补光面板会以小窗形式悬浮在屏幕上。
    3. 此时您可以切换到系统相机或其他任何App，补光悬浮窗会持续存在。
    4. 您可以通过双指捏合来缩放悬浮窗大小，或拖动它到屏幕的四个角落。

1.2 拍摄模式

在主界面底部，您可以左右滑动切换不同的拍摄模式：

  • 拍照：拍摄高质量的静态照片。
  • 拍Live：拍摄包含声音和动态效果的Live Photo（实况照片）。
  • 录像：长按拍摄按钮开始录制视频，松开即可结束。
  • 延时拍照：提供倒计时功能，方便您进行自拍或合影。

1.3 补妆镜

  • 操作：从主屏幕进入 “更多” 页面，点击 “补妆镜”。App会立即启动一个无任何UI干扰的全屏镜像视图，利用前置摄像头让您清晰地整理妆容。

1.4 九宫格

  • 操作：在 “更多” 页面的“拍摄辅助”区域，轻点 “九宫格” 行即可开启或关闭。开启后，相机预览界面会显示辅助构图线，帮助您拍出更具美感的照片。

二. 模拟体验工具

我们内置了多款有趣的模拟互动工具，为您带来片刻的宁静与乐趣。

2.1 电子木鱼

  • 敲击：在“电子木鱼”界面，点击屏幕中央的木鱼即可进行敲击，屏幕上会显示“功德+1”等祝福语动画。
  • 自动敲击：点击底部的 “自动敲击” 按钮，木鱼将根据您设定的速度持续敲击。再次点击可停止。
  • 自定义：点击右上角的齿轮图标进入设置。在这里，您可以：
    • 更换木鱼样式：在“选择木鱼”区域，横向滑动并点选您喜欢的材质和外观。
    • 管理祝福语：点击“管理祝福语”，可以添加、删除或重置敲击时显示的文字。
    • 设置背景：使用“背景”下方的分段控件，可在“关闭”、“音乐”或“视频”模式间切换。选择“视频”模式后，可点击“添加”从您的相册导入视频作为背景。

2.2 电子佛珠

  • 拨动：在“电子佛珠”界面，用手指向下滑动屏幕即可模拟拨动佛珠的动作，同时累积计数。
  • 计数规则：每拨动108颗佛珠（一轮），“累计圈数”会自动加一，并伴有提示音。
  • 自定义：点击右上角的齿轮图标进入设置。您可以更换佛珠的材质样式（如小叶紫檀、和田玉等）、开启或关闭“功德+1”弹幕动画，以及设置背景音乐或视频。

2.3 电子烧香

  • 敬香：点击底部的 “敬香” 按钮，香会开始燃烧，并伴有缓缓升起的烟雾效果。屏幕上会实时显示香的剩余燃烧时间。
  • 自定义：点击右上角的齿轮图标进入设置。您可以选择不同的香（如檀香、藏香等），并同样可以设置背景音乐或视频。

三. 其他实用工具

3.1 滚动字幕

  • 操作：在 “更多” 页面中，点击 “滚动字幕”，进入设置页面。
  • 自定义：您可以输入想要显示的文字，并通过滑块和颜色选择器调整滚动速度、字体大小、文字颜色和背景颜色。设置完成后，点击 “开始滚动” 即可在全屏模式下展示，非常适合在演唱会、接机等场合使用。

3.2 手电筒

  • 快速开关：在 “更多” 页面中，轻点“手电筒”行即可快速打开或关闭闪光灯。
  • 亮度调节：当手电筒开启时，长按“手电筒”行，会弹出一个“调整亮度”的选项。点击后即可进入亮度调节页面，通过滑块在1%到100%之间精细控制亮度。

常见问题 (FAQ)

Q: 为什么手电筒功能需要相机权限？

A: 在iOS系统中，闪光灯（Torch）是相机硬件的一部分，因此任何需要控制闪光灯的应用都必须获得相机权限。我们承诺仅使用此权限来开关手电筒，绝不会捕获任何图像。

Q: 我选择的背景视频会上传到服务器吗？

A: 绝对不会。您从相册选择的所有视频都仅在您的设备本地进行处理和播放，我们不会也无法访问、上传或存储您的任何媒体文件。

Q: 如何在App内找到用户协议和隐私政策？

A: 您可以在“更多”页面进入“设置”，在设置列表的底部可以找到“用户协议”和“隐私政策”的入口。

Welcome to LumiCamera! This is a comprehensive application that integrates a variety of creative shooting and utility tools, designed to provide you with a fun and convenient user experience. This guide will walk you through all the features of the App.

1. Camera & Fill Light

1.1 Fill Light Mode

  • Activation & Adjustment: On the main shooting screen, the screen itself acts as a fill light. Simply tap anywhere on the screen to bring up the control panel at the bottom. You can adjust the lighting effect in real-time by dragging the "Brightness" and "Opacity" sliders.
  • Color Control: In the control panel, you can freely select colors using the color wheel in the "Custom" tab, or quickly apply well-designed atmospheric colors from the "Preset Colors" tab.
  • Floating Fill Light (Picture-in-Picture):
    1. Switch to the "Floating Window" tab in the control panel.
    2. Tap the "Enable Floating Window" button. The fill light panel will appear as a small floating window.
    3. You can now switch to the system camera or any other app, and the fill light window will remain on top.
    4. You can resize the window by pinching with two fingers or drag it to any of the four corners of the screen.

1.2 Shooting Modes

At the bottom of the main screen, you can swipe left or right to switch between different shooting modes:

  • Photo: Capture high-quality static photos.
  • Live Photo: Capture Live Photos with motion and sound.
  • Video: Press and hold the shutter button to start recording a video; release to stop.
  • Timer: Set a countdown for hands-free selfies or group photos.

1.3 Makeup Mirror

  • Operation: From the home screen, navigate to the "More" page and tap "Makeup Mirror". This will launch a full-screen, UI-free mirror view using the front camera, perfect for a quick touch-up.

1.4 Grid Lines

  • Operation: In the "Shooting Assistance" section of the "More" page, tap the "Grid" row to toggle it on or off. When enabled, compositional grid lines will be displayed on the camera preview to help you frame better shots.

2. Simulation Tools

2.1 Digital Woodfish

  • Knocking: On the "Digital Woodfish" screen, tap the woodfish in the center to knock. An animation with blessing texts like "Merit +1" will appear.
  • Auto-Knock: Tap the "Auto-Knock" button at the bottom to have the woodfish knocked continuously at your set speed. Tap again to stop.
  • Customization: Tap the gear icon in the top right to open settings. Here you can:
    • Change Woodfish Style: In the "Select Woodfish" section, swipe horizontally and tap on your desired style.
    • Manage Blessings: Tap "Manage Blessings" to add, delete, or reset the texts that appear when knocking.
    • Set Background: Use the segmented control under "Background" to switch between "Off," "Music," or "Video." For video mode, tap "Add" to import a video from your photo library.

2.2 Digital Prayer Beads

  • Scrolling: On the "Digital Prayer Beads" screen, swipe down firmly on the screen to simulate scrolling the beads and accumulate counts.
  • Counting Rule: Each time you complete a round of 108 beads, the "Rounds" counter will increment with an alert sound.
  • Customization: Tap the gear icon to access settings. You can change the bead material (e.g., Red Sandalwood, Jade), toggle the "Merit +1" danmaku animation, and set a background with music or video.

2.3 Digital Incense

  • Lighting: Tap the "Light Incense" button to start burning the incense, complete with rising smoke effects. A timer will display the remaining burn time.
  • Customization: In the settings panel, you can choose different types of incense (e.g., Sandalwood, Tibetan Incense) and set a background with music or video.

3. Other Utilities

3.1 Scrolling Ticker

  • Operation: On the "More" page, tap "Scrolling Ticker" to enter the setup screen.
  • Customization: You can input your desired text, adjust the scrolling speed, font size, text color, and background color. Once set, tap "Start Scrolling" to display it in full-screen mode, perfect for concerts or airport pickups.

3.2 Flashlight

  • Toggle: On the "More" page, tap the "Flashlight" row to quickly turn the torch on or off.
  • Brightness Control: When the flashlight is on, long-press the "Flashlight" row to reveal an "Adjust Brightness" option. Tapping it will take you to a settings page where you can finely control the brightness from 1% to 100% using a slider.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why does the flashlight feature require camera permission?

A: In iOS, the flashlight (torch) is part of the camera hardware. Therefore, any app that needs to control the torch must obtain camera permission. We promise to use this permission solely for toggling the flashlight and will never capture any images.

Q: Will the background videos I select be uploaded to a server?

A: Absolutely not. All videos you select from your photo library are processed and played locally on your device. We cannot and will not access, upload, or store any of your media files.

Q: How can I find the User Agreement and Privacy Policy within the app?

A: You can go to the "More" page, then "Settings". You will find the entries for "User Agreement" and "Privacy Policy" at the bottom of the settings list.
